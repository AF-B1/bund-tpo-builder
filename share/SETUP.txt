BUND TPO BUILDER
================

Nothing to install. Works on Windows with Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.

1. Unzip this folder anywhere.
2. Double-click:  START HERE.bat

That's it. The app opens in your browser.

KEYS
----
↑ ↓   move price and print
→     next period
←     previous period
Enter show full profile
Delete erase letter
R     reset session

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
If START HERE.bat does nothing, double-click Bund-TPO-Builder.html instead.
